Present Progressive.

Excuse me, Mr. Never heard of it, what is it?

Do not worry, it is very simple, we just need to read the following meaning:

Español

El "present progressive" es un tiempo verbal que se utiliza para hablar sobre acciones que están sucediendo en el momento presente. También se le conoce como "presente continuo". Se forma usando el verbo "to be" en presente (am, is, are) seguido del verbo principal en su forma "-ing" (gerundio).

English

The "present progressive" is a tense that is used to talk about actions that are happening in the present moment. It is also known as "present continuous". It is formed using the verb "to be" in the present (am, is, are) followed by the main verb in its "-ing" form (gerund).

Are you talking about we need to know how to use the verb "To Be" in first place?, I forgot everything about it...

It is OK!, because we can see it in a short way right now, it is very easy!

Now, see some rules you need to know for "Present progressive" after the verb TO BE.

Add -ing to the end of a verb.

  • Eat Eating
  • Sleep Sleeping
  • Work Working
  • Cook Cooking
  • Read Reading

Verbs ending in consonant + e. Remove the -e and add -ing.

  • Create Creating
  • Practice Practicing
  • Smile Smiling

Consonant + vowel + consonant.

  • Sit Sitting

Change -ie to -y and add -ing

  • Die Dying
  • Lie Lying

Verb with two or more syllables and final syllable IS STRESSED, double the final letter.

  • Begin Beginning
  • Forget Forgetting
  • Lie Lying

-ING Spelling
  • Be Being

Consonant + vowel + consonant. Do not double w-x-y

  • Play Playing
  • Fix Fixing
  • Snow Snowing
